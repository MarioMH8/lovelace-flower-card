## Info

 - **Origin**: Africa
 - **Production**: China
 - **Category**: Crassulaceae, Cotyledon
 - **Blooming**: Succulent plants, blooms sometimes, with orange small flowers
 - **Color**: Leaf color green, with adequate sunlight, leaves edges turning to red brown
