## Info

 - **Origin**: Africa
 - **Production**: China
 - **Category**: Moraceae, Ficus
 - **Blooming**: Foliage plant, sometimes can open flower
 - **Color**: Leaf color green

## Maintenance

 - **Size**: Diameter≥10cm，Height≥20cm
 - **Soil**: Loose fertile sandy loam
 - **Sunlight**: Enjoy sufficient sunlight and resistant to half shade
 - **Watering**: Like wet, water thoroughly when the soil surface is dry
 - **Fertilization**: Dilute fertilizers following instructions, apply once monthly in spring and autumn
 - **Pruning**: Remove old, diseased and dead leaves timely
