 - **Origin**: Asia
 - **Production**: China
 - **Category**: Crassulaceae, Pachyphytum
 - **Blooming**: Succulent plants, blooms sometimes, a long scape sprouts from the plant center, blooms with small red flowers in 5-pointed star shape
