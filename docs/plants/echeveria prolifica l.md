## Info

 - **Origin**: Yunan
 - **Production**: China
 - **Category**: Crassulaceae, Echeveria
 - **Blooming**: Succulent plants, sometimes flowers, orange flowers
 - **Color**: Leaf color pale green, with adequate e sunlight, lower leaves turning to pink
