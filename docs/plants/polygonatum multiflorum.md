## Info

 - **Origin**: China
 - **Production**: Hunan, Jiangxi, Anhui, Hubei, Guangdong
 - **Category**: Liliaceae, Polygonatum
 - **Blooming**: Flowering period May-June, fruiting period August-October
 - **Color**: Flower color white green, fruit color black
