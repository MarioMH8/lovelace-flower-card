## Info

 - **Origin**: Asia
 - **Production**: Yunnan, Taiwan, Guizhou
 - **Category**: Bignoniaceae, Radermachera
 - **Blooming**: Flowering period May-September, fruiting period October-December
 - **Color**: Flower color white, light yellow

## Maintenance

 - **Size**: Diameter ≥ 20 cm, height ≥ 20 cm
 - **Soil**: Peat or soil with specific nutrients
 - **Sunlight**: Like adequate sunshine, resistant to half shade
 - **Watering**: Water thoroughly after soil surface dries
 - **Fertilization**: Apply adequate base fertilizers before transplanting, apply liquid fertilizers once to twice monthly then
 - **Pruning**: Remove old, diseased and dead leaves timely
