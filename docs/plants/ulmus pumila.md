## Info

 - **Origin**: China
 - **Production**: Lower Yangzte River Valley
 - **Category**: Ulmaceae, Ulmus
 - **Blooming**: Flowering and fruiting period March-June
 - **Color**: Flower color yellow, fruit color light green
