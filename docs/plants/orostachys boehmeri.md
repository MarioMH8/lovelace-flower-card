## Info

 - **Origin**: Japan
 - **Production**: China
 - **Category**: Crassulaceae, Orostachys
 - **Blooming**: Succulent plants, sometimes flowers
 - **Color**: Leaf color gray green to blue green

## Maintenance

 - **Size**: Diameter ≥ 3 cm, height ≥ 5 cm
 - **Soil**: Peat and akadama mixed in 3:1 ratio
 - **Sunlight**: Likes adequate light, dormant in winter, shade properly under summer strong light
 - **Watering**: Resistant to drought, less water to avoid overgrowing
 - **Fertilization**: Dilute fertilizers following instructions, apply once every 1-2 months
 - **Pruning**: Remove dead and rotten part timely
