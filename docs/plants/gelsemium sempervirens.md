## Info

 - **Origin**: Southern United StatestoCentral America
 - **Production**: Taiwan, Guangdong
 - **Category**: Loganiaceae, Gelsemium
 - **Blooming**: October-April
 - **Color**: Flower color yellow

## Maintenance

 - **Size**: Diameter ≥ 20 cm, height 20-30 cm
 - **Soil**: Peat or soil with specific nutrients
 - **Sunlight**: Likes sunshine, put in sunny places, avoid strong sunlight in summer at noon
 - **Watering**: Water thoroughly when soil is dry, avoid saturated water
 - **Fertilization**: In flowering stage, apply fertilizers with N-P-K ratio about 15－20－25
 - **Pruning**: Remove dead and diseased branches timely
