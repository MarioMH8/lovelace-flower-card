## Info

 - **Origin**: Holland
 - **Production**: Yunnan
 - **Category**: Araceae, Anthurium
 - **Blooming**: Flowers year around with spathe flower, viewing period 2-3 months
 - **Color**: Leaf color green , bract pink purple
