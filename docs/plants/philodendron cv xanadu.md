## Info

 - **Origin**: South American tropical areas
 - **Production**: Southern China
 - **Category**: Araceae, Philodendron
 - **Blooming**: Foliage plants, sometimes flowers, spathe flowers
 - **Color**: Leaf surface green, lower surface and stem red
