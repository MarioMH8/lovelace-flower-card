## Info

 - **Origin**: China
 - **Production**: Southern and NorthWestern China
 - **Category**: Rutaceae, Zanthoxylum
 - **Blooming**: Flowering period March-May, flowering period July-September
 - **Color**: Flower color light yellow green, fruit color green, brown
