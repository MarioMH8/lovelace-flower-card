## Info

 - **Origin**: Yunan
 - **Production**: China
 - **Category**: Crassulaceae, Sedum
 - **Blooming**: Succulent plants, sometimes flowers, light yellow flowers
 - **Color**: Leaf color yellow green, when sunny leaf color turning to red, green if not sufficient
