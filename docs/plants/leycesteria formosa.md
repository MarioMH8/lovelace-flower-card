 - **Origin**: Asia
 - **Production**: Sichuan, Guizhou, Yunnan
 - **Category**: Caprifoliaceae, Leycesteria
 - **Blooming**: Flowering and fruiting period May-October
 - **Color**: Flower color white or pink, sometimes with fuchsia
