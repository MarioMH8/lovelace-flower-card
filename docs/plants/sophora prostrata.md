## Info

 - **Origin**: Subtropical and temperate areas
 - **Production**: Southwestern China
 - **Category**: Leguminosae, Sophora
 - **Blooming**: Flowering period May-July, fruiting period August-December
 - **Color**: Flower color light purple

## Maintenance

 - **Size**: Diameter ≥ 15 cm, height ≥ 25 cm
 - **Soil**: Peat or soil with specific nutrients
 - **Sunlight**: Like sunshine, grows in sunny places
 - **Watering**: Water thoroughly when soil is dry, avoid saturated water
 - **Fertilization**: Dilute fertilizers following instructions, apply 1-2 times monthly
 - **Pruning**: Remove and shape excessive branches
