## Info

 - **Origin**: Holland
 - **Production**: China
 - **Category**: Crassulaceae, Echeveria
 - **Blooming**: Succulent plants, blooms sometimes, blooms with white flowers in May-pointed star shape, red flower buds
 - **Color**: Leaf color emerald green , light purple
