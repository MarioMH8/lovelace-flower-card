## Info

 - **Origin**: Mexico
 - **Production**: China
 - **Category**: Crassulaceae, Sedum
 - **Blooming**: Succulent plants, sometimes flowers with a short period flowering time 2-3 days
 - **Color**: Leaf will turn pink red with sufficient sunlight
