## Info

 - **Origin**: Mexico
 - **Production**: China
 - **Category**: Crassulaceae, Echeveria
 - **Blooming**: Succulent plants, sometimes flowers with a short period time
 - **Color**: Leaf margin will turn pink under sufficient sunlight
