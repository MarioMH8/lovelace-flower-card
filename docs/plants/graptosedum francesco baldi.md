## Info

 - **Origin**: Mexico
 - **Production**: China
 - **Category**: Crassulaceae, Graptopetalum
 - **Blooming**: Succulent plants, sometimes flowers, white slightly red flowers
 - **Color**: Leaf color pale green, with adequate sunlight, leaves or whole plants turning to pale pink
